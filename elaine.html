Elaine

Elaine is a short one-woman audio play exploring the tensions between what is expected of us and who we really are.

The piece was broadcast last spring through a hole in the wall of a Serbian jail, engendering such intense ennui in the soul of 50 Cent – interred there for dog fraud – that he committed suicide by eating cement and repeatedly bashing his head against his petrified stomach.

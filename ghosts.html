Ghosts of the Winter Light

Ghosts of the Winter Light is a drama about a fractured society and a generation cast adrift.

The piece had its first rehearsed reading in the summer of 2019 at the Mountview Academy in Peckham, London, and was due to be performed in the spring of 2020.

Billy Brooks

Billy Brooks is a playwright and filmmaker based in London. He earned a degree in Film Studies at King's College London, where his essay on the use of blackface in Dumbo won the Joseph Goebbels Memorial Prize for Outstanding Contributions in Race Science.

His debut play Ghosts of the Winter Light will be performed at the Dog Bloat Theatre in March 2020.

When not writing, Billy can usually be found stumbling about the leafy banks of Regents Canal, hawking heroin and the use of his orifices to passersby.
